NAME
       magnus - A very simple screen magnifier

SYNOPSIS
       magnus [--about] [--refresh-interval=MILLISECONDS]

DESCRIPTION
       A very simple screen magnifier for visually impaired users.  Allows
       setting the zoom level to anything between 2x and 5x.

OPTIONS
       --refresh-interval=MILLISECONDS
	      How often to update Magnus's magnifier view. Defaults to 120ms.
